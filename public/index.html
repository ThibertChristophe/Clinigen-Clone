  1. CLINIGEN DIRECT

    Clinigen Direct is a globally available service which helps clinicians, pharmacists and pharmacy technicians source hard to find medicines by connecting them with our specialist customer services team, either online or by phone (and even fax).

  2. PHARMATCEUTICAL & BIOTECH COMPANIES

    Clinigen is the global leader in the specialist management of ethical, regulatory compliant and efficient solutions to help pharmaceutical and biotech companies provide access to medicines.

  3. OUR MISSION

    Between two and five billion people have low or non-existent access to the medicine they need. We don’t just help make medicines available, we get them to the people who need them. All over the world and however challenging the circumstances.

  4. HEALTHCARE PROFESSIONALS

    Clinigen provides healthcare professionals around the globe with ethical, compliant and timely access to treatments that are unlicensed or unavailable at the point of care.

Our mission is simple

RIGHT MEDECINE.
RIGHT PATIENT.
RIGHT TIME.

Clinigen’s mission is to deliver the right medicine to the right patient at the right time, to improve the quality of people’s lives around the world. There are only three ways for a patient to ethically access a medicine: through a clinical trial, unlicensed or commercial route and we are the only company to globally manage access across all three routes.
